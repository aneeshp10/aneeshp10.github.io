Great to see you here!

I am Aneesh Patil, a 20-year old thinker majoring in Computer Engineering,  Computer Science and a minor in Business (if the B-school enrollment process permits)  at the University of Wisconsin-Madison.

At UW-Madison, I'm striving to gather knowledge in distinct facets ranging from Computers to the Stock Market in hopes to create products that improve people's livelihood. Over the last two years at college, I've worked closely with technology and computers, as well business operation and finance. I've developed not only as a software engineer, but also as a thinker through the vast number of projects I've worked on. I'm passionate towards solving real-world problems using efficient software solutions and that's what we at UniTrack are currently working on. Since my first semester at college, I've spent most of free time building on UniTrack , a 'one-stop' productivity platform for the UW-Madison community that suggests tailor-made study spots based upon campus library's crowdedness in real-time.

In addition to that, my current work is spread across as an undergraduate researcher involving low-power systems to estimate water penetration levels in soil, a consultant for an invest-tech platform launching a new product, and a global citizen regularly providing essential goods to the underprivileged during these trying times.
